Business Principles

Long-term Thinking

Given a business's current position in the market, what is needed for that business to maintain a competitive advantage and grow as economic conditions and consumer trends change? Which systems that we create now are still going to be viable 5, 10, 20 years down the road?

Friendly and Professional

Anyone is a potential client or future business associate. Therefore I strive to maintain a positive and professional attitude always - in public, private, and on social media.

Learn From the Competition

As a business it's helpful to look at the market from the perspective of a customer. Why choose the services of one business over another? Important factors include presentation, customer service, price, and clarity. Pay attention to what the competition does that works, and what doesn't work.

Don't be Afraid of Taking Risks

To succeed we often have to try out many things. Some work, some don't. The more things we try the more things we find that work. When we do something that doesn't work out we need to learn from our mistakes and move on, keeping losses to a minimum.
